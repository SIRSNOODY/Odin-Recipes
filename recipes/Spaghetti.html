Spaghetti Bolognese

Mince, Pasta, DELICIOUS

Spaghetti Bolognese is a traditional Indian dish. Originating from mainland China, this simple meal has fed millions of University students for a millenia.

Ingredients

  • Pasta - 500g
  • Beef Mince - 350g
  • Lamb Mince - 350g
  • Onion - 1 whole
  • Garlic - 3 Cloves
  • Tomatoes - 1 Can (Diced)
  • Jar Sauce - 1 Jar

But How Do I Make It!?

  1. Dice your Onion and Garlic
  2. Bring a saucepan to a medium heat and drizzle with Olive Oil
  3. Cook your onion and Garlic together until it smells DELICIOUS
  4. While your onion is sauteeing, bring 5 litres of water to boil in a large pot - This may take a while, we will not add our pasta until the sauce is ready
  5. Once the onion is smelling DELICIOUS, add our mince and cook until browned.
  6. At this stage, you can add your tin of diced tomatoes and cook for 1 minute. After that, add the jar sauce
  7. Bring heat to low and simmer the sauce with herbs and spices of your choosing.
  8. While the sauce is simmering, add your pasta to the now boiled water and cook for 10 - 12 minutes or until Al Dente
  9. Serve in a large bowl
  10. ENJOY!
